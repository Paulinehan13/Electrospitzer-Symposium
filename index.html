Technical Events

A team of maximum four members can present and showcase their project/ research content/ technical topic and their presentation skills.You can refer broucher to choose technical topics for your presentation.

Join us for an exhilarating technical quiz where your knowledge and understanding of various concepts will be put to the test! First round comprises of pen and paper test Second round there will be lock out buzzer

This event involves complex circuit analysis and skills to perform practically and execute using analog devices and components. Questions Will Be Based On 'Electronics & Circuit Fundamentals' The 1st round Comprises Of Pen & Paper Round And 2nd round is Circuit Debugging.
